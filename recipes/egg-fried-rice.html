Egg Fried Rice

How to make Delicious Golden Egg Fried Rice!

Make this delicious yet simple recipe with just a few ingredients at home! A nutritious yet filling dish that can be made in just minutes!

Note: Did you know, leftover rice is preferred when making fried rice dishes as it is firmer and unlikely to stick for a better eating experience and texture.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Cooking Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Prawns
  • Peas

Steps

  1. Have leftover rice ready, and if not, freshly cooked rice is fine, but is generally less preferred.
  2. Crack some eggs into a bowl, and whisk until beaten well into a smooth and runny consistency.
  3. Heat the pan to low-medium heat, and start cooking the prawns until halfway cooked.
  4. When the prawns are halfway cooked, add the rice and start mixing well to prevent it clumping together.
  5. Add Soy Sauce and mix well together with the rice and prawns.
  6. Once the soy sauce has been mixed, add the peas and mix with the other ingredients in the pan
  7. Once everything looks cooked, finish cooking with Sesame oil.
