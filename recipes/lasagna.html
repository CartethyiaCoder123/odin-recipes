How to make Delicious Lasanga at Home!

When I was a wee lad, my grandmama used to make me some banging lasagna at the comfort of our home. Today I want to share this wonderful recipe with others to spread the love of my grandma's cooking to family and individuals!

Ingredients ❨ Serves One ❩

To put together this dish, we are going to need a few things. To be honest, I have never made lasagna before and I'm just guessing

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Ground Beef
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Beat some eggs into a bowl
  2. Use Flour to somehow make lasagna sheets
  3. Mix Baking powder with flour for some rising for whatever reason
  4. Cook up the ground beef and season to taste
  5. Assemble it all together then slap cheese on top
  6. Serve and garnish with Basil
