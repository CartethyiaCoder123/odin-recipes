How to Make Instant Noodles

Maybe this is your first time ever eating instant noodles, which is somehow impressive. Anyways here is a straightforward recipe for making your first ever instant noodles.

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodles ❨ Pick a flavour you like ❩
  • Pot or Microwave to boil and cook noodles
  • Scissors to cut flavouring packets

Steps

  1. Open your noodle packet and place it into a bowl
  2. Pour the amount of water specified on the back of your noodle packet
  3. Pour your flavouring packets now or after the draining the water, depending whether it is soup noodles or dry noodles. Look at the back of the noodle packet for more details.
  4. Microwave or use a kettle to boil the water to cook the noodles
  5. Wait for it to cook. The noodles will be loose and separated when done.
  6. Drain water if necessary and serve
